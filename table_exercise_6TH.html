Table1.Benefits and risk of regular exercise in diabetes
Benefits Risks
  • Potentially lower A1C in children and adolescents with type 1 diabetes.
  • Reduced risk of CVD,hypertension,colon cancer,obesity and osteoporosis.
  • Increased overall life expectancy.
  • Increased CV endurance,muscle fitness and flexibility.
  • Increased whole body insulin.
  • Enhanced self-esteem and sense of well-being.
  • Hyperglycemia.
  • Hypoglycemia.
  • Muscluoskeletal injury.
  • CV accident(angina,myocardial infarction,dysrhythmia,sudden death).
  • Deterioration of under lying retinopathy and nephropathy.
